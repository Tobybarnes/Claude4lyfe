## Multi-Source Synthesis

**New Capability:** Weekly updates now pull from 5 data sources:
1. **Slack** - Team channel updates
2. **Google Email** - Carl feedback, SLT communications
3. **Signal MD Files** - Strategic observations logged
4. **Vault** - Project data, milestones, reviews
5. **Figma** - Design work and artifacts

### Why Multi-Source Matters

**Single-source updates miss strategic context:**
- ‚ùå "Team shipped Autopilot UX" (just Slack)
- ‚úÖ "Autopilot positioned in Tobi email as AI-first commerce priority (Email), connects to 'agentic UI patterns' signal from Jan 15 (Signal), shipping against Vault project #8590 (Vault), designs reviewed with Carl last week (Figma link)"

### Synthesis Patterns

**Connect the dots across sources:**
- Email from Carl + Slack update = Strategic priority with execution progress
- Vault milestone + Signal observation = Why this work matters now
- Figma link + Vault project = Visual proof of strategic work
- Slack discussion + Email feedback = Leadership engagement and team response

**Example:**
```markdown
### Autopilot: Positioned Shopify's AI-First Commerce Strategy
- sources: Email (Tobi, Feb 7), Slack (#proj-growth-agent), Vault (#8590), Signal (AI/agentic-ui-patterns), Figma (Autopilot UX R2)
- why: Tobi's email positioned this as strategic bet on AI commerce. Connects to broader agentic UI trend we're tracking. Team delivered on milestone 3 days early.
```

## Quick Self-Review Questions

Before publishing, ask:

1. **Strategic Impact:** Does each Big Rock show strategic value, not just progress?
2. **Your Leadership:** Is YOUR contribution visible, or just team activity?
3. **Business Metrics:** Does each item have quantifiable impact?
4. **Visual Excellence:** Does the update showcase the design work visually?
5. **Merchant Impact:** Is it clear how this helps merchants succeed?
6. **Carl's Priorities:** Would Carl see what he cares about (design excellence, merchant outcomes, strategic contribution)?
7. **Operational Noise:** Have I removed task-level details that dilute strategic narrative?
