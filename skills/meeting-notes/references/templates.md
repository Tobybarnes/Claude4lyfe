# Meeting Notes Templates

## Recap Structure

```markdown
## Meeting Recap: [Meeting Title]

**Date:** [Meeting Date]
**Attendees:** [Participants from transcript]
**Duration:** [If available]

### TL;DR
[2-3 sentence executive summary of the meeting]

### Key Discussion Points
1. **[Topic 1]** - [Brief summary of discussion and any decisions]
2. **[Topic 2]** - [Brief summary]
3. **[Topic 3]** - [Brief summary]

### Decisions Made
- [Decision 1]: [Context and rationale]
- [Decision 2]: [Context and rationale]

### Open Questions
- [Question that wasn't resolved]
- [Topic that needs follow-up discussion]
```

## Signal Entry Format

Location: `$MYEP_PATH/Signals/[Category]/YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`

```markdown
# YYYY-MM-DD - [Brief Title]

**Category:** [Signal Category] (Internal/External)
**Source:** Meeting - [Meeting Title]

**Observation:** [What was discussed/noticed]

**Context:** [Meeting context, who mentioned it]

**Implications:** [Why it matters strategically]

**Connections:** [Related signals, projects, or themes]

---
```

## Action Items Format

```markdown
### Action Items

| Owner | Action | Due | Status |
|-------|--------|-----|--------|
| [Name] | [Task description] | [Date if mentioned] | [ ] |
| [Name] | [Task description] | [Date if mentioned] | [ ] |

### Follow-up Meetings
- [Any scheduled or proposed follow-up meetings]

### Commitments Made
- **[Person]** committed to [what they agreed to do]
- **[Person]** will [their commitment]
```
